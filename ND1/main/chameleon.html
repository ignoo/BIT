Panther chameleon

Furcifer pardalis
Originates from eastern and northern Madagascar.
They are one of the most colorful of all chameleons, particularly the male chameleon.

Veiled chameleon

Chamaeleo calyptratus
Originates from Saudi Arabia and Yemen.
They are known for their variable color changes due to a variety of factors, including to show aggression, social status, reproduction, and stress.

Jackson's chameleon

Trioceros jacksonii
Originates from East Africa.
They are sometimes called three-horned chameleons because males possess three brown horns.

Parson's chameleon

Calumma parsonii
Originates from eastern and northern Madagascar.
The Parson's chameleon is considered the world's largest chameleon by weight and one of the largest by length.

Brookesia nana

Brookesia nana
Originates from northern Madagascar.
They are the smallest chameleon species and the world's smallest reptile.
